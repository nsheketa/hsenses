ABOUT H zentre

A BREATH OF WELLNESS

Located on Middle Road in Tsim Sha Tsui, H zentre is a single complex that innovatively embodies food & beverage, wellness and retail trade mixes within a one tower development.

The first development in Hong Kong that puts wellness and health in its forefront, H zentre provides purpose-built systems and facilities for medical centre and hassleless clinics for doctors.

Capitalising on the vibrant backdrop of Tsim Sha Tsui, together with large-scale and ongoing development in the neighbourhood, H zentre is no doubt the place to be.

Designed for efficiency

Designated lobbies & lifts for different zones:
6 lifts in total are arranged to serve separated lift zones for the Wellness and Food & Beverage Floors;
2 designed lifts serve the multi-floors public carpark.

Care for health

Separate exhaust fan with non-return damper to communal exhaust duct is purpose-built for each unit on Wellness floors (for 2/F to 6/F, 8/F to 12/F).

Remarkable headroom

Ceiling height (floor to floor) of 4.9m for each typical floor, giving comfortable room of space and airiness.
