ABOUT H SENSES

A BREATHE OF WELLNESS

Located on Middle Road in Tsim Sha Tsui, H Senses is a single complex that innovatively embodies food & beverage, medical and retail trade mixes within a one tower development.

The first development in Hong Kong that puts wellness and health in its forefront, H Senses provides purpose-built systems and facilities for medical centre and hassleless clinics for doctors.

PRESTIGIOUS PANORAMA

H Senses is situated in a dynamic location with 270 degree Victoria Harbour view. Premium restaurants and eateries occupy the top floors, fully utilising the superb scenic advantage.

Design for efficiency

Designated lobbies & lifts for different zones: 6 lifts in total are arranged to serve separated lift zones for the Wellness and Food & Beverage Floors; 2 lifts serve the multi-floors public carpark.

Care for health

Separate exhaust fan with non-return damper to communal exhaust duct is purpose-built for each unit on Wellness floors (for 3/F to 7/F, 9/F to 15/F).

Remarkable headroom

Ceiling height (floor to floor) of 4.9m for each typical floor, giving comfortable room of space and airiness.
