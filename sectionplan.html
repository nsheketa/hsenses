wellness

the heart of well-being

1: TAILORED FOR EFFICIENCY

The Wellness zone features purpose-built builder’s works for specialist medical equipment, including recessed floor for medical equipment and wiring installation (for 2/F to 6/F, 8/F to 12/F). Public carpark has direct and quick access to the Wellness floors (for 2/F to 6/F).

2: UNPAralLeded quality

The Wellness floors are designed to achieve and exceed professional standards with the following features:
- Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) of ‘excellent’ grade for common area is to be achieved
- Individual primary air unit of fresh air intake for each Wellness floor (for 2/F to 6/F, 8/F to 12/F)
- Substantial floor loading for heavy medical machineries (for 8/F and 9/F)
- Central medical waste collection room

3. reliable power supply

Aiming to give you a peace of mind, emergency cases are also taken care of with the following purpose-built systems (for 2/F to 6/F, 8/F to 12/F):
- Emergency Generator for equipment backup
- Emergency Power Supply sockets for medical equipment or drug refrigerators
